Hobbies and Interests

Rock Climbing

I've been rock climbing for about five years now. I started climbing in a local gym, then moved to outdoor climbing about three years ago. I mostly climb in the Alberta rockies; however, I have also climbed at a few locations in Tahoe and Yosemite! Climbing for me has been a great oppurtunity to explore the outdoors and see places I never would be able to just hiking.

Snowboarding

Snowboarding has been part of my life since I was a young kid. I was lucky to grow up in a cold snowy city with three small ski resorts within the city limits. I first started to learn during school field trips to the local hills, from there, it clicked. The -20 degree weather, five hour long drives, and sleepless nights are always worth a good day of snowboarding.

Hiking

I didn't grow up being outdoorsy. I went on my first hike while in university, wearing jeans, converse, and I brought a single 500-ml water bottle. Since then, I've learned a couple things and have grown an appreciation for the outdoors. By far, my favorite adventures have included a three day backpack on the Alta Via 1 in Italy. However, the trail I believe everyone should expereince once would be the Lake O'Hara Alpine circuit in British Columbia. Lake O'Hara has some of the best trails I have ever seen. But because of the high demand it needs to be planned months in advance due to limited bus space!

Baking

I started to get very good at cake decorating when I worked at a cake shop for about three years in high-school. Since then, I have always enjoyed it because I loved sharing the things I made with my friends and family. Baking things from scratch also means its healthy because I didnt buy it from a store (haha). Mostly I love baking cheesecake and cheescake cupcakes. But recently I have been getting into baking breads!
